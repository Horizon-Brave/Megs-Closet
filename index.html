The same pigment never reads the same twice. Below is your selected shade on a range of undertones from fair to deep — the lightness and warmth underneath shift the result, exactly the way it happens at the mirror.

A bright shade stays vivid on fair skin but turns deeper and richer on darker skin — that difference is the underlying tone doing the work, not a filter on top.

Shade names and color values in this lab are original and were created for this tool — not taken from, and not reproducing, any brand's proprietary names or palettes.
